Welcome to the Help & Support Center

Purpose of the App

Our app is designed to help you track anything in your daily life, whether you're working to build positive routines, monitor behaviors, or simply keep tabs on various activities. With the ability to log and report on the frequency and timing of your actions, you gain clear insights into patterns and trends. The app lets you review your activities over daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly timeframes, offering a comprehensive view of your consistency and progress in any area you choose to monitor.

How to Use the App

Adding a Habit

Start by creating a new habit from the home screen by pressing the + button on top. Alternatively, a new habit can also be created from the Settings menu. You can choose an activity name, custom icon, and color for the habit. The habit/activity could be anything that you want to count. It doesn't matter what.

Habit Types: Positive and Negative

When adding or editing a habit, the app tries to auto-detect whether it's a positive or negative habit, but you can also manually select it. This helps in personalizing your trend summary and reflections based on the type of habit you're tracking.

Logging Habits

Once your habits are set up, simply tap on them to log an entry. Each log captures the time and date for easy reference. You can track various habits simultaneously. Long pressing the icon will give options to undo the last log and catchup on missed.
